Business Model

Free software · Paid expertise · Recurring revenue

The software is free.
The expertise is the product.

LSH follows the same model that built Red Hat ($34B), MySQL, Elastic, and WordPress VIP: give away the open-source platform, and sell the knowledge, time, and reliability that users cannot easily provide themselves. Every Victron installer, KNX programmer, or homeowner who runs LSH is a potential support client — because 30+ integrations mean 30+ things that can need professional attention.

You do not need to own the software. You need to be the person who knows it best in your market.

Switching costs work in your favour
  • Client's HomeKit home is built on your LSH config — reconfiguring on another platform takes days.

  • VPN access, monitoring, and alerting are all managed by you. Leaving means losing that oversight.
  • Annual renewal friction is near zero — most clients auto-renew without a conversation.

Go-to-Market Roadmap & 3-Year Revenue Projection

Action Plan

From first deployment to stable MSP income

4-phase growth roadmap
1

Months 1–2 · Build credibility

Deploy LSH at home and at one friend's house for free. Document both setups with photos and a short case study. This is your portfolio — the only credibility signal that matters in this market is a live working installation a prospect can see.

2

Months 3–4 · First paying clients

Target Victron owners in your area (solar forum posts, Facebook groups, local Victron dealer referrals). Offer a fixed-price €600 "Victron + HomeKit setup" to the first 3 clients. Include a 12-month Professional SLA in the invoice — frame it as the handover guarantee, not an upsell.

3

Months 5–9 · Build the recurring base

Grow to 8–12 SLA clients through referrals. Introduce the Managed Hosting tier for clients who want a branded dashboard URL. Standardise your onboarding checklist and support runbook so each new client takes less of your time than the last.

4

Year 2+ · Scale and specialise

Pick a vertical: holiday villas (Bayrol + AuxAir + Somfy), commercial energy (Victron + SolarEdge + Waveshare), or residential luxury (KNX + Denon + HomeKit). Specialisation raises your hourly rate and makes referrals targeted. Train a junior to handle tier-1 tickets while you sell and architect.

The key insight
Home Assistant has 3 000 integrations and a huge community. That means clients can find answers on Reddit without calling you. LSH has 30 carefully built integrations — you are the community. That's not a weakness: it's your moat. Clients pay for access to the person who built and maintains their specific setup.
